# Marketing

This folder contains resources to support outreach and marketing for the X-CITED Digital Community.

## Contents

- **[institutions.md](institutions.md)** — A directory of digital communities and facilities across the four GW4 institutions (Cardiff, Bristol, Bath, Exeter), with contact information and website links. Use this for targeted outreach when advertising events, recruiting speakers, or growing the community membership.

## Purpose

The marketing resources in this folder are intended to help the X-CITED Digital Community team:

- Identify potential new members and communities to invite
- Reach relevant digital facilities and research groups across GW4
- Maintain an up-to-date contact list for event promotion and collaboration
