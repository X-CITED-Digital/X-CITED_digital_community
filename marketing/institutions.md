# GW4 Institutions Directory

This directory lists digital communities and facilities across the four GW4 institutions — University of Bath, University of Bristol, Cardiff University, and University of Exeter — relevant to the X-CITED Digital Community outreach and networking efforts.

Each institution section contains two tables:
- **Digital Facilities** — research infrastructure, specialist labs, and platforms
- **Digital Communities** — groups, networks, and communities of practice

> 📝 Where contact details are not yet confirmed, fields are marked *TBC*. Please update as contacts are established.
