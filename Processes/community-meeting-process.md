# Community Meeting Process

This document describes the process for organising and running the **X-CITED Digital Community of Practice** bimonthly meetings, along with a reusable checklist.

## Overview

Meetings are held **every other month** (approximately six times per year) as online webinars via Microsoft Teams.

When scheduling meetings:

- Avoid dates that coincide with school/university closures or religious holidays to be inclusive.
- Vary the day of the week and time of day across meetings so that part-time colleagues can attend different sessions.

## Meeting checklist

Use this checklist for each meeting cycle. Copy it and track progress in the [planning spreadsheet](https://universityofexeteruk.sharepoint.com/:x:/r/sites/XCITEDDigitalCommunityofPractice-Organisationteam/Shared%20Documents/Organisation%20team/CoP%20Planning.xlsx?d=wdd201644d91a47bfbf46158b49c8d101&csf=1&web=1&e=SMrMDi).

### At least 6 weeks before the meeting

- [ ] Identify a topic and speaker for the session.
- [ ] Send a speaker invitation using the [speaker invitation template](templates/speaker-invitation-email.md).
- [ ] Confirm speaker availability and agree a date/time (avoiding closures, public holidays, and religious observances; vary day and time from the previous meeting).
- [ ] Update the planning spreadsheet with confirmed date, time, and speaker details.

### At least 3 weeks before the meeting

- [ ] Finalise the webinar details (topic title, speaker bio, session format).
- [ ] Create the webinar event in Teams:
  - Open the **Calendar** app in Microsoft Teams.
  - Select **New webinar**.
  - Complete the event details using the [webinar event template](templates/webinar-event-template.md) as the description.
  - Add the speaker and planning team members as **presenters / tech support**.
- [ ] Publish and share the webinar registration link.
- [ ] **Advertise the event across GW4:**
  - [ ] Post in relevant GW4 channels and networks. *(add specific channels/lists here as the community grows)*
  - [ ] Send the event details to **Nicola** to forward to the X-CITED mailing list.
  - [ ] Post in the X-CITED Digital Community Teams channel.
  - [ ] *(add any further advertising venues here)*

### 1 week before the meeting

- [ ] Reach out to the speaker to confirm they can still attend and share any logistics (joining link, agenda, timing).
- [ ] Prepare the welcome/intro slides:
  - Welcome and introduction to the X-CITED Digital Community (for any new attendees).
  - Updates on X-CITED events and funding opportunities.
  - Speaker introduction.
  - Brief outline of the session: talk → Q&A → community discussion on the topic to facilitate networking and knowledge sharing.
- [ ] Prepare QR code linking to the X-CITED mailing list sign-up page (to display at the end of the session).
- [ ] Prepare or update the feedback form link.

### On the day

- [ ] Open the Teams webinar and admit attendees.
- [ ] Deliver the welcome/intro slides.
- [ ] Introduce the speaker.
- [ ] During the talk: monitor the chat for questions.
- [ ] After the talk: thank the speaker and open for Q&A.
- [ ] Display the mailing list QR code and invite attendees to join.
- [ ] Facilitate the community discussion in the second half of the session.
- [ ] Share the feedback form link in the chat.

### After the meeting

- [ ] Send a follow-up message in the Teams channel (recording link if available, feedback form, any resources shared).
- [ ] Review feedback form responses and note any actions for future meetings.
- [ ] Update the planning spreadsheet with outcomes and notes.
- [ ] Begin planning the next meeting.
